Biography

    I am a Ph.D. candidate at Centre for Artificial Intelligence (CAI), University of Technology Sydney (UTS), advised by Dist. Prof. Jie Lu and Prof. Guangquan Zhang. I was a research intern with the AI Residency Program at RIKEN Center for Advanced Intelligence Project (RIKEN-AIP), working on the robust domain adaptation project with Prof. Masashi Sugiyama, Dr. Gang Niu and Dr. Bo Han. I visited Gatsby Computational Neuroscience Unit at UCL and worked on the hypothesis testing project with Prof. Arthur Gretton, Dr. Dougal Sutherland and Wenkai Xu. I have served as a senior program committee (SPC) member for ECAI and program committee (PC) members for NeurIPS, ICML, CIKM, ECAI, FUZZ-IEEE and so on. I also serve as reviewers for many academic journals, such as TPAMI, TNNLS, TFS, TCYB, AMM and so on. I received the UTS-FEIT HDR Research Excellence Award (2019), Best Student Paper Award of FUZZ-IEEE (2019) and UTS Research Publication Award (2018).

Research Interests

    My current research interests lie in domain adaptation (including transfer learning) and two sample testing. My long-term goal is to develop intelligent systems that can learn knowledge from massive related but different domains (domains drawn from different distributions) automatically. Researching on domain adaptation and hypothesis testing is the first step for achieving my long-term goal.
